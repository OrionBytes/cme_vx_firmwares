"Music in general is heading towards a massive "virtualization" process where everything becomes part of a software application. Sure, those nice graphical interfaces never get dusty nor scratched but there is no way of sending (and receiving!) good, uncompromised vibes and energy through a computer display. As long as we have to use our body and fingers to communicate and tell good stories through music, well-crafted musical instruments and gear will always make a difference. Keyboard controllers are a crucial part of any studio and live rig and everyone in this business knows how hard it is to find real good ones. CME solved my quest: sturdy, solid, straight-ahead good feelings on the keyboard and a good backup of faders and knobs. With a great look and a fair price, the UF serie made the operation of choosing a midi controller a real no-brainer."

- Alberto Rizzo Schettino
