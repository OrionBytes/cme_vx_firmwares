Z-Key 49/61/76/88 Master Keyboard

The Z-Key Master Keyboard - Designed For the Discerning User who rates Quality over Price.

With more than 18 years of experience, CME now present to you a brand new product incorporating our latest technology - the Z-Key Master Keyboard series. Z-Key is available in 4 sizes – 49/61/76/88 notes to meet all requirements.

  • Designed for the discerning user

Z-Key's range has a high perceived value and is designed for the discerning user's demands. It incorporates a completely new keybed design, resulting in better, more consistent action.

  • The most comfortable piano-style key

Z-Key is the first keyboard range in the world with piano-style Zenith keys throughout the complete range. Zenith keys are the most comfortable piano-style keys following several years research by CME. Each key is individual, not manufactured as part of a block, so that they can be mounted individually.

  • Z-Keys are robust

The casing is manufactured in high quality aluminium for extra strength and the end panels in Piano-lacquered PE.

Keybed Framework:

Each key is individual so can be adjusted independently

The contact surface of Z-Key black key is up to 20% more than other keyboards

Specification:

- 49/61/76/88 Zenith keys, weighted action with velocity sensitivity and after-touch
- Pitch bend and modulation wheels
- 1 Data Dial
- 1 Standby Button
- 1 MIDI OUT
- Octave+ and Octave- buttons
- Sustain pedal jack socket
- Choice of velocity curve and aftertouch curve
- USB bus-powered, Class-compliant with Windows XP/Vista/7 and Mac OS X.  

Features:

- Professional semi-weighted keyboard with after-touch across the entire range of Z-Key series keyboards
- Assignable data dial
- Choice of velocity curve and aftertouch curve
- USB bus-powered, Class-compliant with Windows XP/Vista/7 and Mac OS X. Alternatively can be powered by Mains/USB converter (not included) if not connected to PC  
- Z-Key retains the excellence of all CME products, and its improved key sensitivity makes it feel like a real piano
- Unlike keyboards with integrated keys, each key on Z-Keys is individual so can be adjusted individually for touch and response
