Henrik Klingenberg / Sonata Arctica

                              

 

“ I first heard about the Widi X8 when Labyrinth were supporting my band Sonata Arctica on our latest European Tour. Their keyboard player Alex spoke very highly of it, and while I had some doubts about if it was the thing for me, he quickly proved that all my fears where totally misplaced. Seeing it work perfectly night after night convinced me so I just had to get a pair myself. For the last 10 years I've been using Roland shoulder keyboards and it's a bit annoying trying to run around on stage with a long cable getting in the way.  Finally that is over for me and I really look forward to playing the big festivals this summer where I can really take full advantage of the freedom the Widi X8 offers “ Henrik Klingenberg  / Sonata Arctica
