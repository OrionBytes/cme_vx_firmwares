ASX-Advanced Synthesis Expansion Card

 

The ASX combines all CME UF and VX series keyboards with a latest state-of-the-art synthesis engine. It provides aUSB connection for remote control and configuration, a microphone input, stereo line and stereo headphone outputs.

The ASX stores up to eight DSP software plug-ins and can load and perform any one at a time. Three plug-ins are shipping with every ASX board: the wild Minimax, the inspiring LightWave and the charismatic B4000. Number four comes as a free online registration bonus: download and install the Vocodizer, another awesome musicial tool in amazing quality.

MiniMax is the highly acclaimed replica of the famous legend. Incredibly fat and responsive sound, intuitive control... tweaking its knobs is pure fun, a real performance synth!

 

While the oscillators of the LightWave are based on wavetables, the aftermath is virtual analog - giving you wide control of the sound. From complex pads to powerful leads, LightWave offers a vast range of sonic possibilities.

The B4000 turns your CME into the Queen of Organs. The genuine sound of a real living electromechanical 400 pound monster. Lively, authentic, emotional...


The Vocodizer is one of the most flexible vocoders ever developed - plug in your microphone and enjoy spectacular,voice modulated sounds...

The ASX comes with quick reference templates which fit your CME UF and VX series keyboard. This is the one for MiniMax...

The ASX plug-ins, precisely model the synthesis process of the real synth which allows expression and access to all aspects of the sound and its modulation. you will find the sound to be much more dynamic, richer, more detailed and more lively.

The ASX Editor Software for Windows and Mac OSX provide the different plug-in GUIs and access to all sound settings. Presets and Preset Banks can easily be managed, saved to Preset Files, and uploaded to the ASX hardware - allowing you to store your personal favorite sounds on the ASX board.
