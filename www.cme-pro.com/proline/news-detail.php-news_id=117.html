CME attend Dealer Days in Poland - June 2011
1773 views since 2011-06-30

CME Europe were invited to attend the annual Dealer Days with our Distributor in Poland, Audiostacja.

The Dealer Days were held at a modern Conference Centre on the outskirts of Warsaw in an attractive location overlooking a large lake.

“The purpose of the Dealer Days is for us to get to know our customers, spend time with them on a social level, not just talking business.” explained Piotr Dygasiewicz. “We are in a ‘people business’ in which our Dealers have to trust us.  It is through this trust that we have grown in just 5 years to become the leading MI Distributor in Poland.”

Audiostacja also invite a select number of their suppliers to the Dealer Days and this year CME Europe were one of only 6 companies invited. CME recognise the growth of potential business in Poland and were honoured to be asked. 

The suppliers had the opportunity to make a presentation, giving the gathering gathe chance to see where their company had come from and what their plans were for future growth.

The Dealer Days ended with an open air evening banquet. As might be expected, many of the guests provided live music well into the night. Guest artist was Reggie Worthy the renowned bass guitarist.
