Antonio Dixon
 

Antonio Dixon began his professional songwriting and production career while attending Hampton University, in Virginia. This Denver, Colorado native, worked with numerous artist including Wrecks-n-Effects, GUY, and Teddy Riley. He then moved to Chicago where he broadened his songwriting to include gospel. His credits in the gospel arena include working with such recording artists as: Marvin Sapp, R. Kelly, Yolanda Adams and artist Smokie Norful. He currently resides in Los Angeles where he has recorded with various artists including:
Celine Dion
Toni Braxton
Christina Aguilera
Britney Spears
Babyface
Brian McKnight
N' Sync
Justin Timberlake

Future projects include: 3LW, Tweet, Justin Guarini, (American Idol) and Craig David. Continue to be on the lookout, for up and coming phenomenal songwriter/producer.
